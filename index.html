About Me

Hi, you can call me "Hally" and I'm located in Toronto, Ontario .

I want to build excellent web pages and might as well some software that can improve people's objectives. It's really cool to turn all those ideas into virtual reality, eh?

However, art is another world to me and so is technology, but then merging these two worlds can absolutely benefit me to build a masterpiece. Creating and designing a website or any kind of applications are fun though, but programming can be really challenging because there is always a lot of problem-solving going on. But to me, solving even the smallest bug can give me a rewarding feeling.

When I am not coding, I sometimes do whatever comes in to my mind. It could be like watching movies/tv-shows, reading, or sleeping just because I get bored.
